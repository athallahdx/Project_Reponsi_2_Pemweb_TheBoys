Billy Butcher is the fearless and unyielding leader of The Boys, a covert group determined to expose and dismantle the corrupt and destructive influence of superheroes, particularly The Seven and their corporate backers, Vought International. Born and raised in London, Butcher's gritty upbringing and sharp wit shaped him into a natural leader, capable of rallying his team even in the direst situations.


Driven by a personal vendetta against Homelander, Butcher harbors an intense hatred for all Supes, stemming from a tragedy involving his wife, Becca. This relentless pursuit of vengeance often blurs the lines between heroism and obsession, making him a complex and morally ambiguous figure. Despite his ruthlessness, Butcher's charisma and deep loyalty to his team anchor The Boys, uniting them under his vision of justice, however brutal it may be. With his tactical brilliance, unmatched determination, and an arsenal of unconventional methods, Butcher has become the ultimate anti-hero.
