Hughie Campbell is the moral compass and emotional core of The Boys, embodying the humanity and vulnerability that often contrasts with the group’s ruthless tactics. A small-town guy with big dreams, Hughie’s life takes a dark turn when his girlfriend, Robin, is tragically killed in a reckless act by A-Train, a member of The Seven. This devastating event propels Hughie into the chaotic and dangerous world of Vought International and their corrupt superheroes.


Initially hesitant and overwhelmed, Hughie grows into his role as a vital member of The Boys, guided by his innate sense of justice and his drive to protect the innocent. His relationship with Starlight, a Supe who defies Vought’s corruption, challenges his black-and-white view of the world, proving that not all Supes are beyond redemption.
